My Design Projects

Established in 2010, Hollywood Burger is the creation of an investment private equity group who decided to mix Hollywood with a fast-food restaurant concept. Built from scratch, this brand focuses on a fun family atmosphere, fresh food, and much like its name "hollywood culture". The project included designing all of its materials, from the website, to packaging, to its t-shirts.

Needing to be noticed, Hortonworks reinvigorated their brand by tearing down the old to establish a new look. This modern, clean brand has awarded them much attention as the expert in the Hadoop field. Everything from the logo, the website, to its collaterals were redesigned.

Seeking to gain an edge over its competitors, HKD Snowmakers, injected some cheekiness to its brand by radically changing the way they market themselves. By using a business to consumer approach, HKD stands out above other competitors in several snowmaking trade journals. This project included multiple trade ads, revised website, trade show banners, and collateral materials.

Acclaim, A small-time home healthcare service looking to attract more business; decided to revamp their entire look. Targeting to better suit their market, this new brand features new modifications. These include large photography of it's patients in it's brochures, a newly designed logo, and prominent roll-up banners.
